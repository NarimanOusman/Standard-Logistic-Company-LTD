About Us

Standart Logistics Group, based in South Sudan, is a professional
travel and logistics company committed to delivering reliable and
high-quality services.Their mission is to provide seamless and
efficient solutions that cater to both individual and corporate
clients.The company offers a wide range of services including
hotel bookings, taxi services, car rentals and hiring, event
management, touring and travel arrangements, as well as national
and international ticket booking.With a strong focus on hospitality
and customer satisfaction, Standart Logistics Group ensures that
every client receives personalized attention and a smooth travel
experience from start to finish.
